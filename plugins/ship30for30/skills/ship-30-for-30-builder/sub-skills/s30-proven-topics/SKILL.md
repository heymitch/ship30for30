# Day 24: Doubling-Down On Proven Topics

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 24" or "Proven Topics"

## What You'll Build

Develop a repeatable strategy for writing 100+ variations on one proven topic by combining it with tangential sub-topics and different angles.

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's best-performing topics
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D24-content.md` for Cole's case study + frameworks
3. **Customize** — Identify student's 1 best topic and 3-5 tangential angles
4. **Apply framework** — Use "A Super of 1 Is A Super of 9" to expand variations
5. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 24 row in student's Notion database
   - Fill the **Template** column with topic expansion strategy
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
6. **Fallback** (no Notion): Save to `workspace/day-24-topic-strategy.md`
7. **Confirm** — "Your topic strategy is ready. Write about your proven topic today, changing 1 variable."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their content, check:
- Uses data to identify which topic to double-down on
- Has clear strategy for varying the topic (audience, approach, outcome, format)
- Understands testing one variable at a time (science experiment mindset)
- Committed to 100+ repetitions with fresh angles

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Shipped". Celebrate the flywheel effect: more publishing = more data = better understanding = bigger audience.
