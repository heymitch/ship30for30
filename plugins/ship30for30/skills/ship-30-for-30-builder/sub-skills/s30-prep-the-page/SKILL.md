# Day 15: Prep The Page

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 15" or "Prep The Page"

## What You'll Build

A structural skeleton for an essay with headline, introduction, 3-5 mapped subheads, and final takeaway—before writing body content.

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's niche, topics, and Notion DB ID
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D15-content.md` for Prep The Page system and checklist
3. **Customize** — Help student choose a topic and map structure using working headline formula
4. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 15 row in student's Notion database
   - Fill the **Template** column with their prepped skeleton
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
5. **Fallback** (no Notion): Save to `workspace/day-15-template.md`
6. **Confirm** — "Your Prep The Page skeleton is ready. Structure looks solid—now write the body content."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their prepped page structure, check:
- **Headline specificity** — Not generic ("Why This Matters") but specific and benefit-driven
- **Subhead alignment** — Each subhead directly answers a question the headline raises
- **Visual scanning** — Reading only headline + subheads reveals the full argument without body content

Word count: Structure only (no body content needed yet)

Give 1-2 specific notes on headline clarity or subhead matching.

---

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Structure Mapped" (or note in config). Direct to writing body content. Ask if they want to move to Day 16.
