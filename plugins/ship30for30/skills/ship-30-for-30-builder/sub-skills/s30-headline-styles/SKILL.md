# Day 12: 5 Proven Headline Styles

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 12" or "Headline styles"

## What You'll Build

Write 5 headlines for one topic using all 5 proven styles (List, Story, Opinion, Question, Framework), then publish one as your Atomic Essay.

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's niche, topics, and Notion DB ID
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D12-content.md` for Day 12 writing template
3. **Customize** — Fill template with student's topic/niche from config
4. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 12 row in student's Notion database
   - Fill the **Template** column with customized template
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
5. **Fallback** (no Notion): Save to `workspace/day-12-template.md`
6. **Confirm** — "Your Day 12 template is ready in [Notion/local file]. Write your draft, then tell me when you're done for feedback."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their draft, check:
- **Uses all 5 styles** — Headlines cover List, Story, Opinion, Question, and Framework categories (not just 1-2)
- **Each headline is specific to their niche** — Headlines are tailored to their audience, not generic examples
- **Each style distinct** — No two headlines sound similar; each uses unique structure from the style category
- Word count (target: ~250, max 300)
- One clear idea, no fluff

Give 2-3 specific, actionable notes. Don't rewrite for them.

---

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Shipped" (or note in config). Ask if they want to move to the next day.
