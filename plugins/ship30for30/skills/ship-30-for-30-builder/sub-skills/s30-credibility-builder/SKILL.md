# Day 04: Personal vs Borrowed Credibility

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 04" or "Establish credibility"

## What You'll Build

Your credibility essay titled "How I Got Interested In {Topic}"—showing your specific entry point, perspective shift, and either personal lived experience OR borrowed authority from experts (or both).

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's topic
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D04-content.md` for credibility framework
3. **Decide credibility path** — Help student choose: Personal (lived experience) or Borrowed (expert frameworks)
4. **Customize** — Fill template with specific moment, perspective shift, and what they've learned
5. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 04 row in Notion
   - Fill **Template** column with essay
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
6. **Fallback**: Save to `workspace/day-04-credibility.md`
7. **Confirm** — "Your credibility story is ready. Write your essay showing how you got interested and what shifted. Tell me when done."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their draft, check:
- **Uses personal OR borrowed credibility**: One is clearly chosen and demonstrated
- **Specific proof**: Not "I'm an expert" but "I built X, achieved Y, or studied Z"
- **Not generic**: Avoids "I'm passionate about helping people" type statements
- Clear moment when their understanding shifted or deepened
- Credit given if borrowing from others (authentic blending)

Give 2-3 specific notes on credibility strength.

Update Notion Status to "Shipped". Ask if they're ready for Day 05 (4A Framework).
