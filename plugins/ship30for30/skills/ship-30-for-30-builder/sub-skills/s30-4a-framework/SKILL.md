# Day 05: The 4A Framework

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 05" or "Apply 4A Framework"

## What You'll Build

Your framework essay titled "This Is The Best Book I've Ever Read On {Topic}"—demonstrating understanding of all four 4A paths (Actionable, Analytical, Aspirational, Anthropological) applied to your topic.

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's topic
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D05-content.md` for 4A Framework breakdown
3. **Map 4A paths** — Help student identify one real book/resource representing each path:
   - Actionable (How-to): practical steps
   - Analytical (Why): data and reasoning
   - Aspirational (Transformation): inspiration and growth
   - Anthropological (Psychology): human behavior
4. **Customize** — Fill template with ONE book and five reasons it exemplifies the framework
5. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 05 row in Notion
   - Fill **Template** column with essay
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
6. **Fallback**: Save to `workspace/day-05-4a.md`
7. **Confirm** — "Your 4A analysis is ready. Write your essay on [Book Title] with five reasons. Tell me when done."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their draft, check:
- **All 4 types represented**: Does it reference or demonstrate Actionable, Analytical, Aspirational, Anthropological?
- **Distinct angles**: Each reason is different (not overlapping)
- **Applied to their niche**: Not generic book recommendations but specific to their topic
- Clear understanding of how the book embodies one or more 4A paths
- Specific examples from the resource

Give 2-3 specific notes on 4A understanding.
