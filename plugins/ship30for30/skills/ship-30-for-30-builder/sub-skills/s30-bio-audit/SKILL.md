# Day 27: The 4 Biggest Bio Mistakes

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 27" or "Bio Mistakes"

## What You'll Build

Audit your current bio against the 4 biggest mistakes (only listing what you do, being clever instead of clear, hedging authority, outdated visuals) and identify which ones you're making.

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's current bio/profile
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D27-content.md` for the 4 bio mistakes framework
3. **Audit** — Apply checklist to student's current bio and visuals
4. **Identify** — Which 1-2 mistakes are they making?
5. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 27 row in student's Notion database
   - Fill the **Template** column with bio audit results
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
6. **Fallback** (no Notion): Save to `workspace/day-27-bio-audit.md`
7. **Confirm** — "Your bio audit is complete. Tomorrow you'll rewrite it with all 3 elements: Topic, Audience, Credibility."

---

## Review Criteria

When student completes their audit, check:
- Identifies all 4 mistake categories and rates which apply
- Provides specific examples from their current bio
- Has clear list of what needs fixing
- Understands "planting your flag" (claiming a niche, not hedging)

---

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Shipped". Prepare for Day 28 bio rewrite.
