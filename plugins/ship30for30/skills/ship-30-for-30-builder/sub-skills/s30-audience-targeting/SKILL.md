# Day 03: FOR WHO/SO THAT

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 03" or "Define audience targeting"

## What You'll Build

Your positioning statement titled "The Best Way To Get Started Learning About {Topic}"—demonstrating FOR WHO narrowing and SO THAT outcomes with three specific resource recommendations.

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's topic(s)
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D03-content.md` for FOR WHO/SO THAT framework
3. **Guide narrowing** — Help student pick ONE FOR WHO audience and ONE SO THAT outcome
4. **Customize** — Fill template with three resources for that specific audience
5. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 03 row in Notion
   - Fill **Template** column with essay
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
6. **Fallback**: Save to `workspace/day-03-targeting.md`
7. **Confirm** — "Your targeting statement is ready. Write your essay recommending three resources for [specific audience]. Tell me when done."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their draft, check:
- **FOR WHO is specific**: NOT "business owners" but "e-commerce sellers doing $100k-$500k annually"
- **SO THAT is clear**: Not vague ("improve themselves") but concrete ("buy their first home without a co-signer")
- **Not too broad**: Can they defend why they picked THIS audience over others?
- Resources match the audience's actual needs
- Explanation of why each resource fits their specific FOR WHO/SO THAT

Give 2-3 specific notes on audience clarity.

Update Notion Status to "Shipped". Ask if they're ready for Day 04 (Credibility - Personal vs Borrowed).
