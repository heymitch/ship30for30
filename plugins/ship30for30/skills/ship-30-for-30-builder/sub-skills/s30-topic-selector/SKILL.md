# Day 02: The 2-Year Test

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 02" or "Run 2-Year Test"

## What You'll Build

Your topic list titled "3 Topics I Am Exploring In My Life Right Now"—three topics discovered by answering the 2-Year Test questions about your transformation in the past 24 months.

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's context
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D02-content.md` for Day 2 framework and template
3. **Guide through 2-Year Test** — Help student answer:
   - What questions were you asking 2 years ago that you're NOT asking now?
   - What haven't you experienced 2 years ago that you HAVE now?
   - Where were you in life 2 years ago that's fundamentally different now?
4. **Customize** — Fill template with three 2-Year Test discoveries
5. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 02 row in Notion
   - Fill **Template** column with essay
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
6. **Fallback**: Save to `workspace/day-02-topics.md`
7. **Confirm** — "Your three topics are ready. Write your essay explaining these changes and why they're credible. Tell me when done."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their draft, check:
- **2-Year Test applied**: Does each topic come from a real transformation?
- **Specific, not vague**: "How to be healthy" vs. "How I quit sugar and lost 30 lbs"
- **Lived experience**: Can you tell they've actually LIVED this, not read about it?
- Clear explanation of what changed and why they now have credibility
- Topics are distinct (not overlapping)

Give 2-3 specific notes on which topics are strongest.

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Shipped". Ask if they're ready for Day 03 (Audience targeting with FOR WHO/SO THAT).
