# Day 07: Lean Writing Framework

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 07" or "Learn lean writing"

## What You'll Build

Your reflection essay titled "The People Who Have Influenced Me In {Topic}"—plus understanding of the 8-level progression from atomic essays to scalable business.

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's topic and credibility
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D07-content.md` for Lean Writing 8-level progression
3. **Explain progression** — Walk through Levels 1-8 and validation at each level
4. **Customize** — Fill template with three people who influenced their thinking
5. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 07 row in Notion
   - Fill **Template** column with essay
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
6. **Fallback**: Save to `workspace/day-07-lean-writing.md`
7. **Confirm** — "You've completed Week 1. You now have credible topics, audience clarity, multiple content angles, and 30+ ideas. You're ready to write the first 30 essays. Week 2 begins tomorrow."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their draft, check:
- **Three real influencers**: Named people (not generic "experts")
- **Specific impact**: Not "they taught me" but "they showed me [specific insight]"
- **Connected to credibility**: How their influence shaped their writing authority
- Shows understanding of learning progression
- Personal reflection on why this person matters to their topic

Give 2-3 specific notes on influence clarity.

---

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Shipped". Week 1 complete. They're ready for Week 2 (distribution and daily writing). Archive this week in tracker.
