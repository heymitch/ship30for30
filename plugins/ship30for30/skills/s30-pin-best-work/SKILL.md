# Day 29: Pinning Your Best Work

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 29" or "Pin Content"

## What You'll Build

Identify and pin your best-performing content to X (Twitter) and LinkedIn profiles as your strategic first impression for new visitors.

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's content performance data
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D29-content.md` for pinning strategy framework
3. **Audit** — Review student's 29 days of content for highest-performing pieces
4. **Select** — Choose 1 piece for X (1 pin max) and 3-5 for LinkedIn featured section
5. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 29 row in student's Notion database
   - Fill the **Template** column with pin strategy rationale
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
6. **Fallback** (no Notion): Save to `workspace/day-29-pin-strategy.md`
7. **Confirm** — "Your pinned content is live. New visitors will see your best work first."

---

## Review Criteria

When student completes their pinning, check:
- X pin is their highest-performing piece (credibility signal) OR best positioning statement
- LinkedIn features range across topics (portfolio effect)
- All pins are still relevant to their current niche
- Profile picture and bio are finalized and aligned with pins

---

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Shipped". Prepare for Day 30 celebration and graduation.
