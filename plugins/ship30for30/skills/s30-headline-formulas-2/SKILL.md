# Day 09: Irresistible Headlines (Part II)

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 09" or "Headline formulas part 2"

## What You'll Build

Write clear headlines by adding an explicit audience: [TOPIC] For [SPECIFIC AUDIENCE ARCHETYPE].

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's niche, topics, and Notion DB ID
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D09-content.md` for Day 9 writing template
3. **Customize** — Fill template with student's topic/niche and specific audience from config
4. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 09 row in student's Notion database
   - Fill the **Template** column with customized template
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
5. **Fallback** (no Notion): Save to `workspace/day-09-template.md`
6. **Confirm** — "Your Day 09 template is ready in [Notion/local file]. Write your draft, then tell me when you're done for feedback."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their draft, check:
- **Explicit audience naming** — Headline names a specific audience archetype, not generic readers ("For beginners," "For investors," etc.)
- **Topic + audience formula applied** — Headline follows [TOPIC] For [AUDIENCE] structure from the lesson
- **Specificity without over-rotation** — Audience is narrow enough to feel personal, not so narrow it excludes related readers
- Word count (target: ~250, max 300)
- One clear idea, no fluff

Give 2-3 specific, actionable notes. Don't rewrite for them.

---

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Shipped" (or note in config). Ask if they want to move to the next day.
