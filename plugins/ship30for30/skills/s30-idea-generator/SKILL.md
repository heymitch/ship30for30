# Day 06: Endless Idea Generator

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 06" or "Generate idea matrix"

## What You'll Build

Your idea matrix essay titled "The Best Piece Of Advice I Was Given About {Topic}"—plus a complete 30-idea matrix combining topic × audience × 4A paths × proven approaches.

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's topic and audience
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D06-content.md` for Endless Idea Generator framework
3. **Run EIG process** — Systematically generate 2+ ideas from each 4A path:
   - Actionable (8 approaches)
   - Analytical (7 approaches)
   - Aspirational (7 approaches)
   - Anthropological (9 approaches)
4. **Build matrix** — Create title + one-liner for 8-12 ideas minimum
5. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 06 row in Notion
   - Fill **Template** column with essay
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
   - (Optional) Create linked database for 30-idea list
6. **Fallback**: Save matrix to `workspace/day-06-ideas.md`
7. **Confirm** — "Your 30-idea matrix is ready. You'll never say 'I don't know what to write.' Tell me when you've reviewed it."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their draft, check:
- **All 4A paths represented**: Ideas span Actionable, Analytical, Aspirational, Anthropological
- **Ideas are specific**: "Business tips" vs. "How to send first sales email as solopreneur"
- **Enough for 30 days**: 8+ ideas minimum (could expand to 20-30)
- Topics × 4A paths × approaches = diverse angles
- NO overlapping ideas (each is a distinct angle)

Give 2-3 specific notes on strongest ideas.

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Shipped". This is their content calendar foundation. Ask if ready for Day 07 (Lean Writing Framework).
