# Day 08: Irresistible Headlines (Part I)

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 08" or "Headline formulas part 1"

## What You'll Build

Write a clear headline combining a specific topic + one of the 30 Proven Approaches (no cleverness, maximum specificity).

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's niche, topics, and Notion DB ID
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D08-content.md` for Day 8 writing template
3. **Customize** — Fill template with student's topic/niche from config
4. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 08 row in student's Notion database
   - Fill the **Template** column with customized template
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
5. **Fallback** (no Notion): Save to `workspace/day-08-template.md`
6. **Confirm** — "Your Day 08 template is ready in [Notion/local file]. Write your draft, then tell me when you're done for feedback."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their draft, check:
- **Clarity over cleverness** — Headline immediately tells reader what the piece is about (not vague/clever)
- **Specificity in topic** — Topic is concrete and specific to their industry/niche (not generic)
- **Proven Approach applied** — Headline uses one of the 30 Proven Approaches structure (list, tips, guide, etc.)
- Word count (target: ~250, max 300)
- One clear idea, no fluff

Give 2-3 specific, actionable notes. Don't rewrite for them.

---

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Shipped" (or note in config). Ask if they want to move to the next day.
