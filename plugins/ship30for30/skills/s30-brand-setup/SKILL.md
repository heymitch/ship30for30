# Brand Design Setup

> **Trigger:** "Set up my brand" or "Design my brand"

## What You'll Build

A personal brand config that styles all your essay screenshots.

---

## Interview Flow

1. **Aesthetic direction** — Ask: "Which vibe fits your writing?"
   - Minimal (white bg, thin accents, lots of whitespace)
   - Bold Editorial (strong colors, heavy type, magazine feel)
   - Soft & Approachable (warm tones, rounded, friendly)
   - Dark Mode (dark bg, light text, moody)

2. **Brand colors** — Offer smart defaults based on aesthetic, let them customize:
   - Primary accent (used for borders, subheads, highlights)
   - Background color
   - Text color
   - Secondary accent (optional)

3. **Typography** — Ask preference, map to Google Fonts:
   - Classic Serif: EB Garamond (heading) + Source Serif 4 (body)
   - Clean Sans: Inter (heading) + DM Sans (body)
   - Modern Geometric: Poppins (heading) + Work Sans (body)

4. **Social handle** — @username for card footer

5. **Avatar URL** — Optional headshot for card header

6. **Save** — Write `s30-design-config.md` to project root

7. **Preview** — Generate a sample card HTML so they can see the result

---

## On Completion

"Your brand is set! Say 'Design my essay' after any day to generate a screenshot-ready card."
