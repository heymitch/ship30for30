# Day 10: Irresistible Headlines (Part III)

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 10" or "Headline formulas part 3"

## What You'll Build

Write master-level headlines by combining Topic + Audience + Outcome to create tension and deliver specific value.

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's niche, topics, and Notion DB ID
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D10-content.md` for Day 10 writing template
3. **Customize** — Fill template with student's topic, audience, and desired outcome from config
4. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 10 row in student's Notion database
   - Fill the **Template** column with customized template
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
5. **Fallback** (no Notion): Save to `workspace/day-10-template.md`
6. **Confirm** — "Your Day 10 template is ready in [Notion/local file]. Write your draft, then tell me when you're done for feedback."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their draft, check:
- **Uses master-level formula** — Headline includes specific topic, explicit audience, AND clear outcome (not just topic + audience)
- **Creates tension/curiosity** — Outcome is specific enough to create a curiosity gap, not generic promises
- **Specific language over vague** — Words like "crash," "destroy," "unlock," "master" over weak language like "learn," "understand"
- Word count (target: ~250, max 300)
- One clear idea, no fluff

Give 2-3 specific, actionable notes. Don't rewrite for them.

---

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Shipped" (or note in config). Ask if they want to move to the next day.
