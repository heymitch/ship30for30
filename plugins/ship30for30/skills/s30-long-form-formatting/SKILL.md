# Day 21: Long-Form Platform Formatting

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 21" or "Long-Form Platform"

## What You'll Build

A complete long-form platform post (400-800 words) for LinkedIn or X/Twitter with strong lead-in, 3 structured body sections, and strategic CTA using one of 5 proven lead-in patterns.

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's niche, topics, and Notion DB ID
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D21-content.md` for 3-part anatomy, 5 lead-in patterns, platform-specific rules
3. **Customize** — Help student write lead-in, structure body with 1/3/1 rhythm, choose CTA destination
4. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 21 row in student's Notion database
   - Fill the **Template** column with complete formatted post
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
5. **Fallback** (no Notion): Save to `workspace/day-21-long-form.md`
6. **Confirm** — "Your post is done. Lead-in grabs them, body delivers value, CTA drives action. Ready to publish?"

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their long-form platform post, check:
- **Proper long-form structure** — Scannable formatting, strong opening and closing
- **Clear CTA alignment** — CTA directs to one of 5 destinations; matches post content
- **Platform-specific formatting** — Matches LinkedIn (line breaks) or X (threading) standards

Word count: 400-800 words total

Give 1-2 specific notes on lead-in power or CTA clarity.

---

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Long-Form Complete" (or note in config). Celebrate shipping Day 21—Week 3 complete! Ask if they want to continue to remaining days.
