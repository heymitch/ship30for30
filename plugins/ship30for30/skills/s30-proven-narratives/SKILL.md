# Day 26: Doubling-Down On Proven Narratives

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 26" or "Core Narratives"

## What You'll Build

Identify 3-5 core narratives (personal stories, achievements, background, aspirations, fun facts) that become how people know and identify you.

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's personal data/bio
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D26-content.md` for 5-Category Narrative Framework
3. **Customize** — Map student's 3-5 core narratives across framework categories
4. **Analyze** — Which narratives generated engagement/resonance?
5. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 26 row in student's Notion database
   - Fill the **Template** column with core narrative mapping
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
6. **Fallback** (no Notion): Save to `workspace/day-26-core-narratives.md`
7. **Confirm** — "Your core narratives are mapped. Share 1 today and watch it resonate."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their narrative content, check:
- Uses 1+ core narrative from the 5 categories (Personal Stories, Achievements, Background, Aspirations, Fun Facts)
- Narrative is told authentically (not forced)
- Story/achievement is specific and credible
- Avoids generic/oversharing; keeps focused on niche relevance

---

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Shipped". Prepare for bio audit (D27).
