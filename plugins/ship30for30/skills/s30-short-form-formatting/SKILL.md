# Day 19: Short-Form Formatting

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 19" or "Short-Form Formatting"

## What You'll Build

A single piece of short-form content (for social platform) using one of four rhythm patterns, passing the Screenshot Test, and identified by 4 As category.

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's niche, topics, and Notion DB ID
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D19-content.md` for 80/20 framework, Screenshot Test, 4 As system, and rhythm patterns
3. **Customize** — Help student choose an idea, pick a format, and write short-form piece
4. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 19 row in student's Notion database
   - Fill the **Template** column with their formatted short-form
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
5. **Fallback** (no Notion): Save to `workspace/day-19-short-form.md`
6. **Confirm** — "Your piece is tight and scans fast. Clear takeaway. Ready to test it live?"

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their short-form piece, check:
- **Respects platform constraints** — Fits mobile screen without excessive scrolling
- **Proper formatting** — Uses bullets, line breaks, or numbering effectively for scannability
- **Easy to scan on mobile** — First 2-3 lines convey the core idea; supporting details follow

Word count: 80-200 words typical (platform-dependent)

Give 1-2 specific notes on clarity or format impact.

---

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Short-Form Complete" (or note in config). Move to Day 20 (thread formatting). Ask if they want to continue.
