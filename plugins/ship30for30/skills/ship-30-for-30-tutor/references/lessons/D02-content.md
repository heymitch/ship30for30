## Core Concept

The 2-Year Test is a framework for discovering credible topics to write about. Instead of writing about random subjects or guessing what readers want, you look back at the major changes in your life over the past two years. These changes indicate genuine expertise or lived experience. If something significant happened to you in the last 24 months, you have credibility to write about it—because you're not writing from theory, you're writing from experience.

---

## Frameworks

### The 2-Year Test: Three Core Questions

The 2-Year Test asks you three fundamental questions:

**Question 1: What questions were you asking yourself 2 years ago that you're not asking anymore?**

This reveals the problems you've solved or progress you've made. The questions you've moved past indicate expertise or evolution.

Example: Two years ago you might have asked "How do I start a side hustle?" but now you ask "How do I scale my side hustle to 6-figures?"

**Question 2: What hadn't you experienced 2 years ago that you've now experienced?**

This reveals new knowledge, skills, or life changes. The experiences you've now lived through give you credibility to write about them.

Example: "Two years ago I'd never managed a team. Now I manage five people."

**Question 3: Where were you at in life 2 years ago that's fundamentally different now?**

This reveals transformation. Major life shifts (career changes, location changes, relationship status, financial situation) create distinct expertise.

Example: "Two years ago I was broke. Now I make $10k/month from my business."

## Examples

### The 2-Year Test Examples List

Examples of significant 2-year changes (credibility markers):

1. **Become a vegan** - You can credibly write about veganism, nutrition transition, meal planning, restaurant navigation
2. **Started exercising** - You can write about fitness, habit formation, motivation, gym culture
3. **Graduated college** - You can write about education, career transition, job hunting, student debt
4. **Lost money in crypto** - You can write about financial mistakes, risk management, recovery strategies
5. **Quit job on Wall Street** - You can write about corporate life, alternative careers, financial independence
6. **Got married or divorced** - You can write about relationships, life transitions, personal growth
7. **Learned email marketing** - You can write about email, marketing skills, business strategy
8. **Became an empty-nester** - You can write about parenting, life stages, identity beyond kids
9. **Underwent chemotherapy** - You can write about health, resilience, perspective shifts, medical systems
10. **Built a side hustle to 10k/mo** - You can write about entrepreneurship, sales, business growth, time management

## Formulas

### The 2-Year Test Discovery Formula

**[Life Change 2 Years Ago] → [Progression of Learning] → [Current Credibility]**

Pattern:
- Identify what changed in your life in the last 24 months
- Trace the progression of what you learned during that change
- Claim credibility based on that lived experience
- Use that credibility as the foundation for your writing topics

### The Credibility Indicator Formula

**"Two years ago I [didn't know/hadn't experienced/couldn't do X]. Now I [understand/have experienced/can do Y]. Therefore I can credibly write about Z."**

## Assignment

**Day 2 Assignment:**

Reflect on the past two years of your life and answer the three 2-Year Test questions:

1. What questions were you asking yourself 2 years ago that you're not asking anymore?
2. What hadn't you experienced 2 years ago that you've now experienced?
3. Where were you at in life 2 years ago that's fundamentally different now?

Then, write your atomic essay using the Day 2 template: "3 Topics I Am Exploring In My Life Right Now."

Your essay should:
- Reference the specific 2-year changes you identified
- Present three distinct topics for exploration
- Demonstrate credibility through lived experience
- Connect the changes to why you're writing about these topics

## ChatGPT Prompts

### The 2-Year Test Interview Prompt

**Use this prompt with ChatGPT to generate your 2-Year Test discoveries:**

"I'm writing about my expertise and credibility. Help me complete the 2-Year Test by asking me 10 deep questions about what's changed in my life in the last 24 months. Ask about:

1. Major life decisions I've made
2. Skills I've acquired
3. Problems I've solved
4. Experiences I've had that I hadn't before
5. Questions I've answered that used to confuse me
6. Financial changes
7. Relationship or social changes
8. Career or business changes
9. Knowledge or belief shifts
10. Health or lifestyle changes

After I answer these 10 questions, synthesize my answers into the three core 2-Year Test questions and help me identify my three credibility topics."
