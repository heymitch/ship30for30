# Day 23: The 5 Big Data Points

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 23" or "Data Points"

## What You'll Build

Track 5 engagement metrics (Views, Likes, Comments, Shares, Peer Approval) on your content to identify which topics resonate most with readers.

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's niche and content tracking method
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D23-content.md` for the 5 data points framework
3. **Customize** — Help student audit their past 23 days of content
4. **Analyze** — Identify highest performer in each category (Views, Likes, Comments, Shares, Peer Approval)
5. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 23 row in student's Notion database
   - Fill the **Template** column with 5 data points audit
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
6. **Fallback** (no Notion): Save to `workspace/day-23-data-audit.md`
7. **Confirm** — "Your data audit is complete. Double-down on 1 high-performing topic today."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their data audit, check:
- All 5 data points identified (Views, Likes, Comments, Shares, Peer Approval)
- Top performer in each category clearly named
- Baseline established (can track improvement next round)
- Understands that "all signal is good signal" even for low numbers

---

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Shipped". Prepare for Day 24 topic strategy.
