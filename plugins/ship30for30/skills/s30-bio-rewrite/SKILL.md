# Day 28: Rewriting Your Bio

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 28" or "Bio Rewrite"

## What You'll Build

Craft a "mini sales pitch" bio that answers 3 questions: What topic do you write about? Who is it for? Why should they trust you?

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's credentials and niche
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D28-content.md` for Topic + Audience + Credibility formula
3. **Customize** — Draft 3 versions of student's bio (just topic, topic+audience, topic+audience+credibility)
4. **Review** — Ensure it's CLEAR (not clever) and claims a niche (not hedging)
5. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 28 row in student's Notion database
   - Fill the **Template** column with 3 bio variations
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
6. **Fallback** (no Notion): Save to `workspace/day-28-bio-drafts.md`
7. **Confirm** — "Your bio is rewritten. Post today explaining your new bio positioning."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their new bio, check:
- Clearly states topic (no ambiguity)
- Includes explicit audience OR credibility (or both)
- Avoids all 4 mistakes from D27
- Platform-appropriate length (160 chars for Twitter, expanded for LinkedIn)
- Profile picture and banner updated

---

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Shipped". Prepare for Day 29 pinned content strategy.
