# Day 13: Twitter & LinkedIn Hooks

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 13" or "Platform hooks"

## What You'll Build

Write platform-specific hooks for Twitter and LinkedIn that use proven opener patterns and grab attention in the first line.

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's niche, topics, and Notion DB ID
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D13-content.md` for Day 13 writing template
3. **Customize** — Fill template with student's topic/niche and create platform-specific hooks
4. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 13 row in student's Notion database
   - Fill the **Template** column with customized template
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
5. **Fallback** (no Notion): Save to `workspace/day-13-template.md`
6. **Confirm** — "Your Day 13 template is ready in [Notion/local file]. Write your draft, then tell me when you're done for feedback."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their draft, check:
- **Hooks are platform-appropriate** — Twitter hook uses urgency/line breaks; LinkedIn hook uses 2-sentence rule
- **Uses proven opener patterns** — At least one hook uses Credential, List, Secret, Contradiction, or Experience formula
- **First line grabs attention** — Opening creates immediate curiosity or tension (not generic intro)
- Word count (target: ~250, max 300)
- One clear idea, no fluff

Give 2-3 specific, actionable notes. Don't rewrite for them.

---

## On Completion

Update Notion Status to "Shipped" (or note in config). Ask if they want to move to the next day.
