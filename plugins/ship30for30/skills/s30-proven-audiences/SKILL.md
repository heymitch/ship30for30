# Day 25: Doubling-Down On Proven Audiences

> **Trigger:** "Build Day 25" or "Proven Audiences"

## What You'll Build

Identify explicit and implicit audiences you're reaching, then repeat proven hooks, stories, and examples that resonate across multiple posts (no fatigue risk).

---

## Build Flow

1. **Read config** — Load `s30-config.md` for student's audience data
2. **Read template** — Load `references/D25-content.md` for Two-Audience Typology framework
3. **Customize** — Identify explicit audiences (names/roles) and implicit audiences (sub-groups)
4. **Analyze** — Find which stories/hooks/examples got engagement
5. **Deliver to Notion** (if connected):
   - Find Day 25 row in student's Notion database
   - Fill the **Template** column with proven audience analysis
   - Set **Status** to "In Progress"
6. **Fallback** (no Notion): Save to `workspace/day-25-audience-strategy.md`
7. **Confirm** — "Your audience strategy is ready. Double-down on 1 proven audience today."

---

## Review Criteria

When student shares their audience analysis, check:
- Identifies which audiences are explicit vs. implicit
- Finds 1-2 proven hooks/stories that generated engagement
- Commits to repeating same hook 100+ times (no risk of audience fatigue)
- Understands audience concentration strategy

Update Notion Status to "Shipped". Prepare for Day 26 narrative deep-dive.
